About TruthLens

TruthLens helps you verify facts, identify misinformation, and see the truth beyond the noise — powered by advanced AI.

Our Mission

In a world flooded with misinformation, TruthLens empowers users to critically evaluate the credibility of content shared across social media, news, and public platforms. By combining AI-driven analysis with trusted fact-checking sources, we make truth accessible to everyone.

How It Works

  1. Submit a message, link, image, or video transcript for verification.
  2. TruthLens uses Google Gemini AI to analyze the content’s factual consistency and source credibility.
  3. Get a credibility score, a detailed summary, and trusted source references in seconds.

Why TruthLens?

  • ⚙️ AI-Powered Analysis (Text, Image, Video)
  • 🌍 Supports multiple languages & color themes
  • 🔐 Secure login with Google OAuth
  • 📜 Saves and manages your verification history
  • 💡 Built for awareness, education, and digital responsibility
